LEE, WEN-TING

李文廷

Wen-Tin Lee (李文廷) received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Central University, Taiwan, in 2008. He is currently a Professor in the Department of Software Engineering and Management at National Kaohsiung Normal University. His research interests include software engineering, service-oriented computing, and artificial intelligence.

  • August 2023 - Present Standing Director, Software Engineering Association Taiwan
  • April 2016 - January 2024 Associate Professor, Department of Software Engineering and Management, College of Science and Technology, National Kaohsiung Normal University
  • August 2010 - March 2016 Assistant Professor, Department of Software Engineering, National Kaohsiung Normal University, Taiwan
  • August 2008 - July 2010 Adjunct Assistant Researcher, Software Research Center, National Central University, Taiwan
